BROTHERS BLOOM

Brothers Bloom is a project that I'm currently working on with my partner, Keaton Weber. Together, we are writing, composing, mixing and mastering songs together. We recently released our debut song onto Spotify, called "I'll Be There".


Keaton and I have been friends since high school, and have always shared an interest in music/composition. In high school, him and I would be in the back of the band room, writing melodies and rhythms down that we thought were cool. We would write drum solos and show each other in class the next day. Now, after him and I have furthered out education, we decided to join together and use our skills to make catchy music together!
